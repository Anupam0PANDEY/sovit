1) SANDMAYER'S REACTION:   
ArN2--CuCL/Hcl> ArCl + N2
ArN2--CuBr/HBr> ArBr + N2
ArN2--CuCN/KCN> ArCN + N2

ArN2X--Cu/HCl> ArCl +N2 + CuX
ArN2X--Cu/HBr>ArBr +N2 +CuX
[yield in sandmeyer's Reaction > Gateerman's Reaction]
